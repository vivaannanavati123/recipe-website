Fried Chicken Lasagna Burger

Ingredients:

  • Two pieces of Nashville Hot Chicken
  • A burger patty
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the burger patty
  2. Cover it with marinara sauce and cheese and place it under the broiler
  3. Use the two piece sof fried chicken as the buns and place the patty in the middle
  4. Enjoy!
